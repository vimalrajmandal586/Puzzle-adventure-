## 📖 About This Project
A fully animated story based sliding puzzle game
built with HTML CSS and JavaScript!

Originally inspired by classic puzzle adventure games,
this web version features two exciting game modes,
4 story based levels, bot AI opponent, resume system
and real time data collection via Google Sheets!

No downloads needed — just open and play! 🎮

## 🎮 Game Modes

### 🤖 Player vs Bot
You play against AI computer bot!

Bot Difficulty Levels:
😊 Easy → Bot solves in 8 to 15 seconds
😐 Medium → Bot solves in 4 to 8 seconds
😤 Hard → Bot solves in 1.5 to 4 seconds

Both you and bot solve same puzzle
Whoever gets more points wins!




### 👥 Player vs Player
Two friends play on same device!

Player 1 solves the puzzle first
Then Player 2 solves same puzzle
Whoever solves faster gets more points
Play all 4 levels and compare scores!

## 🚀 How To Play
Step 1 → Open the game link
Step 2 → Choose game mode
Player vs Bot OR Player vs Player
Step 3 → Enter player name(s)
Step 4 → Choose difficulty (PvB mode)
Step 5 → Choose starting level
Step 6 → Read the story
Step 7 → Click Start Puzzle
Step 8 → Slide tiles to arrange them
in correct number order!
Step 9 → Use hints if stuck (3 per level)
Step 10 → Hold Preview to see target
Step 11 → Complete all 4 levels
Step 12 → See final scores
Step 13 → Rate the game!

## 🎯 Scoring System
Each level has a base time bonus:

Level 1 → 100 points base
Level 2 → 150 points base
Level 3 → 200 points base
Level 4 → 300 points base

Points are reduced by:
⏱️ Time penalty → 10 points per 5 seconds
🎮 Move penalty → 5 points per 10 moves

Solve faster with fewer moves
for maximum points!

Minimum score per level → 10 points

Bot gets 15% less points than player



---

## 🏆 Point Examples
Example — Level 1 (100 base points):

Solved in 30 seconds, 25 moves:
100 - (30/5 × 10) - (25/10 × 5)
= 100 - 60 - 12
= 28 points

Solved in 10 seconds, 15 moves:
100 - (10/5 × 10) - (15/10 × 5)
= 100 - 20 - 7
= 73 points

Solve faster = More points! ⚡
